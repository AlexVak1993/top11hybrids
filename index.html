Electric vehicles not only save on petrol or diesel costs, but also have zero emissions. For anyone who hasn’t been completely won over by the advantages of an electric vehicle (EV), a hybrid car is a great alternative - with better economy and lower emissions than cars powered by standard fuel alone.

Most manufacturers are now producing at least one hybrid car in their range, providing a good selection, from chic city cars to solid SUVs, and everything in between.

Take your pick from our list of top 11 hybrid cars that will make your motoring cleaner, more economical and altogether more enjoyable.
